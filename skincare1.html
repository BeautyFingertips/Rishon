Ingredients

Passionfruit Seed Oil

A nourishing natural oil with a high content of skin beneficial components such as vitamin A and C. Vitamin C is known to have an antioxidant effect which helps protect the skin from free radicals and provide skin with a boost of nourishment.

Blend of Natural Oils

A nourishing and exceptionally moisturising blend of castor oil and sunflower oil. This blend is very similar to the fats already present in the skin, which allows these skin comaptible oils to penetrate deeply to help intensely nourish, replenish and protect the skin from within and help leave the skin feeling soft and cared for.

Beeswax

Beeswax is a famous skin care ingredient that helps to soothe, soften and care for the skin by keeping it hydrated, smooth and healthy-looking.

Vitamin E

A powerful antioxidant that protects the skin from free-radical damage, helps to soothe the skin and provides care and protection to the upper layers of the skin.
